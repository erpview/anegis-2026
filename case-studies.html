OPIS FIRMY

BBK S.A od ponad 25 lat zajmuje się importem i dystrybucją artykułów wyposażenia wnętrz w Polsce i za granicą. Jest właścicielem marek home&you i Essex, pod którymi prowadzi sprzedaż produktów dekoracyjnych i tekstyliów do domu.

Firma posiada ponad 130 salonów home&you w Polsce, a pod marką Essex wprowadza kolekcje tekstylne także w Słowacji, Czechach, Rumunii, Węgrzech i krajach bałtyckich.

Opis Projektu

To address this challenge, our team began by researching to understand the needs of the Founderpath team & the Founderpath's users and identify the key features that were important to them. We also looked at existing dashboard apps to see how they were currently being used and to identify gaps in the market that our dashboard could fill.

Once we had a clear understanding of the needs of our users and the features that were important to them, we began the design process of our view. We created wireframes and mockups to visualize the layout and its functionality and then a development team worked on bringing our designs to life.

During the development process, we placed a strong emphasis on user testing to ensure that the app was easy to use for both mobile and desktop users, and intuitive. We conducted multiple rounds of testing with a diverse group of users and made iterative improvements based on their feedback.
